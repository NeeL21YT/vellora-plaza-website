About Vellora Plaza

Our Story

A Vision of Refined Living

Vellora Plaza was born from a singular vision — to create a space where luxury, leisure, and community come together in perfect harmony.

Established in 2018 and spanning over four thoughtfully designed floors including the ground floor, Vellora Plaza stands as the city's most iconic destination for premium retail, gourmet dining, and world-class entertainment. We have welcomed over 10 million visitors and continue to redefine the art of curated living. Our architecture is a masterpiece of contemporary design — soaring atriums flooded with natural light, cascading green walls, and a façade that glows gold at sunset. Every corner of Vellora Plaza has been crafted with intention, from the hand-laid marble corridors to the climate-controlled event lawns on our rooftop terrace.

We believe that great spaces inspire great lives. That belief is the heartbeat of everything we do.
