Getting Here
Depart from Guwahati Railway Station and proceed to Paltan Bazaar bus terminus — the city's central transit hub, a short walk from the station. Board any city bus bound for Six Mile. Vellora Plaza is prominently situated on GS Road, directly below the flyover, approximately 8 km from the railway station — a comfortable 20–25 minute journey by road.
