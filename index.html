Our Story

A Legacy of Refined Living

Vellora Plaza stands as a landmark of architectural grandeur and curated luxury — a destination that transcends shopping and becomes an experience.

Nestled at the heart of the city, Vellora Plaza brings together the world's finest brands, award-winning restaurants, a state-of-the-art multiplex, and world-class entertainment — all within a space designed to inspire and delight. Since our opening, we have redefined what a premium shopping complex means for our community.
